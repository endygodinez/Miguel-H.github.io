Proposal

A person can speak into a microphone and the hardware, which is the raspberry pi, stores the voice into its system, then alters its pitch and tone through a program on python, and outputs the voice in its altered format. The person would speak into the microphone, the hardware would record their voice, and then they have the option to lower or raise their pitch through the program. I want to incorporate a replicator function, which would output a person’s voice in the pitch or tone of another person’s pitch or tone.
